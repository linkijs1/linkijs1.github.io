Homemade Aloe Vera Gel for Skin Care

This simple aloe vera gel recipe is perfect for soothing skin, treating minor burns, or as a natural moisturizer.

Ingredients

  • 2 aloe vera leaves
  • 1 teaspoon vitamin E oil (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon coconut oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash the aloe vera leaves thoroughly.
  2. Cut the leaves open and scoop out the clear gel.
  3. Blend the gel until smooth.
  4. Mix in vitamin E oil or coconut oil if desired.
  5. Store in a clean, airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Tips

Always patch test before applying to your skin. Use within 7 days for freshness.
