Homemade Aloe Vera Gel for Skin Care

This simple aloe vera gel recipe is perfect for soothing skin, treating minor burns, or as a natural moisturizer.

Ingredients

  • 2 aloe vera leaves
  • 1 tsp vitamin E oil (optional)
  • 1 tsp coconut oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash the aloe vera leaves thoroughly under running water.
  2. Cut the leaves open and scoop out the clear gel using a spoon.
  3. Blend the gel until smooth using a blender or food processor.
  4. Mix in vitamin E oil and coconut oil if desired.
  5. Store in a clean, airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.

Tips

Always patch test before applying to your skin. Use within 7 days for freshness.
