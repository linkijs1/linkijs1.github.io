1. Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

Nearly indestructible, this plant tolerates neglect and purifies air efficiently. Perfect for beginners.

2. ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

With glossy leaves and very low water needs, the ZZ Plant is ideal for dark corners and offices.

3. Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

Pothos is a trailing plant that grows well in low light and is easy to propagate in water.

4. Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum)

This elegant plant tolerates shade and blooms white flowers even in low light.

5. Cast Iron Plant (Aspidistra elatior)

True to its name, the Cast Iron Plant is nearly indestructible and perfect for beginners.

6. Chinese Evergreen (Aglaonema)

Chinese Evergreens are colorful, low-light tolerant plants perfect for decorative purposes.

7. Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum)

A pet-friendly option that thrives in indirect light and produces adorable "pups."

8. Dracaena

Dracaena varieties offer dramatic foliage and grow well in shaded areas.

9. Philodendron

With heart-shaped leaves and trailing vines, Philodendron thrives in low to medium light.

10. Parlor Palm (Chamaedorea elegans)

This classic Victorian-era plant loves shade and adds a tropical feel indoors.
