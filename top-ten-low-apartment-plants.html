Top 10 Low Light Apartment Plants

Don’t let low light keep you from enjoying greenery indoors. These 10 houseplants thrive in apartments and shady corners.

1. Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

Nearly indestructible, this plant tolerates neglect and purifies air efficiently. Perfect for beginners.

2. ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

With glossy leaves and very low water needs, the ZZ Plant is ideal for dark corners and offices.
